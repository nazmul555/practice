Book a Tee Time

TeeTimes.net makes it easy for you to find and book golf tee times at some of the best golf courses around the world. Find and compare golf courses across the United States, throughout Canada, in Mexico, Europe, and around the world. Our most popular golf destinations include Palm Springs, Arizona, Las Vegas, Florida, and British Columbia. With more than 2,000 golf courses to choose from, where will you golf tomorrow?

Established in 2008, TeeTimes.net is an online tee time network, providing golfers with an easy way to make golf reservations at their favorite golf courses whether at home or on vacation. We aggregate tee time inventory from wholesalers and barter round providers to help you discover discounted golf while making it easy to book real-time reservations at many of the top golf courses. If you are planning a holiday, we make it easy for you to book multiple rounds of golf from different golf courses with a single, secure transaction. You have lots of other things to worry about when planning a vacation, booking your golf should not be one of them.

To get started, browse for available tee times by location or golf course name, and add the desired tee time to your cart. When ready, book your round(s) securely with our easy to use booking system. Step 2, go golfing.
